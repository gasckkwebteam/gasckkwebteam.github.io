Department of Chemistry

Department Profile

The Department of Chemistry was established in the year 2017 and offer Undergraduate program in Chemistry. The motto of the department is to create awareness among the students to face the challenges of advancing science and technology. The Department typically houses state-of-the-art laboratories, classrooms, and equipment for students to learn and conduct research. The faculty members in our department are experts in their respective fields, and they teach courses ranging from General chemistry to Advanced topics in Organic, Inorganic, Physical, and Nano chemistry. we are creating a platform to the students for developing their subject and social activities by encouraging to participate various conferences, seminars and competitions. Overall, our chemistry department plays a crucial role in training the next generation of chemists and chemical scientists, preparing them for careers in academia, industry, and Government sectors.
